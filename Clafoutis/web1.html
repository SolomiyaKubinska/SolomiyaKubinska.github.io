ABOUT US

Clafoutis – Ukrainian confectionery company producing more than 100 types of high-quality confectionery. The company was founded in 2019. The main production facilities of the company Clafoutis are located in Lviv. The cafe makes every effort to ensure that the products have been of impeccable quality, from the selection of ingredients, the professional approach of skilled confectioners - to the storage and delivery of finished confectionery to the consumer. Feel the home cosiness in our cafe.
